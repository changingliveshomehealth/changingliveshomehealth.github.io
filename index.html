About Us

Changing Lives Home Health

Changing Lives Home Health is based in Glendale, California. We are a Medicare-certified home health agency that continuously cares for and caters to the needs of the elderly, injured, and individuals with chronic disorders.

Sobre Nosotros

Cambiando Vidas Home Health

Cambiando Vidas Home Health esta ubicada en Glendale, California. Somos una agencia de salud en el hogar certificada por Medicare que cuida y atiende continuamente las necesidades de los ancianos, las personas lesionadas y las personas con enfermedades crónicas.

Don’t let asthma limit your adventures

Changing Lives, Home Health has licensed skilled nurses who can provide the appropriate home health care services, working with our client’s physicians. Our nurses provide :

-Healthcare Evaluation and Assessment 

-Wound Care

-Instruction of Safe Use of Oxygen and Respiratory Equipment

-Medication Management

-Patient Education

-Insulin Administration and Diabetic Teaching 

-Incontinence Care

-IV Therapy 

-Enteral Nutrition

-Pre and Post Op Care

-Ostomy Care 

Physical Therapy

-Restoration of Specific Functional Loss, Range of Motion and Strength 

-Adaptive Equipment Teaching

-Rehabilitation Assistance 

-Adaptive Device Teaching 

-Set Up Home Exercise Program 

-Fall and Safety Teaching 

-Assistance and Teaching of Safe Ambulation 

Occupational Therapy

-Therapeutic Exercises and Activities of Daily Living

-Instruction on Orthopedic Devices and Energy Conservation Exercises.

Home Health Aide

-Personal Care

-Ambulation

Speech Therapy

-Assistance in regaining speech abilities that may have been impaired

-Instruction on communication and swallowing techniques

-Intervention for hearing impairment and neurological disorders.

Medical Social Worker

– Assistance in Claiming Financial and Social Resources

– Assistance in Dealing with Social and Emotional Factors Relating to Illness.
